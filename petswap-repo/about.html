How the values work, and what's coming.

Where the numbers come from

Values are pulled automatically from StarPets and AMVGG twice a day and blended together, so no single source's quirks dominate the number you see. Pet images are sourced from AMVGG (real in-game art) rather than marketplace listing photos.

What's next

Account sign-in is planned, so your inventory can live on your account instead of just this browser — tracked automatically as you confirm trades, with your total value kept up to date day to day.

Something look wrong?

Values shift and scrapers occasionally miss things — if a pet looks off, that's useful to know about.

Values on petswap.me are community-driven estimates meant as a starting point for negotiation, not a guarantee of what any trade is "worth." Adopt Me is a trademark of its respective owner; petswap.me is an independent, unofficial fan tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by them.
